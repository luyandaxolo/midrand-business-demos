FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

Can you handle both security and electrical work in one visit?

Yes, absolutely. Our dual-qualified team can assess, quote, and install both your security system and electrical work in a single coordinated project. This saves you time, money, and the hassle of dealing with multiple contractors.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Yes, we offer annual maintenance plans for both security and electrical systems. These include regular inspections, software updates, battery testing, and priority emergency call-outs at discounted rates.

Are your electricians licensed to issue COC certificates?

Yes, all our electricians are ECSA-registered and fully qualified to issue Electrical Compliance Certificates (COC) for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

How long does a typical CCTV installation take?

A standard 4-camera domestic installation takes approximately one day. Larger commercial projects may take 2-5 days depending on the number of cameras, cabling requirements, and network configuration.

Do you install solar systems for load shedding backup?

Yes, we specialise in hybrid solar and battery backup systems designed specifically for South African load shedding conditions. We size your system based on your essential circuit requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Midrand and surrounding areas including Kyalami, Vorna Valley, Halfway House, Noordwyk, Blue Hills, Glen Austin, Carlswald, and beyond. Free site assessments within 15km of Midrand.
