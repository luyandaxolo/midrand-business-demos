Our Story

Who We Are

A locally owned business built on integrity, expertise, and a genuine commitment to our customers.

Motsebone Security and Electrical was founded with a clear mission: to provide Midrand residents and businesses with a single, reliable partner for both security and electrical needs. We saw the frustration of homeowners juggling separate contractors for CCTV and wiring, and we built a company that does both — exceptionally well.

Over the years, we have grown through word-of-mouth referrals and repeat customers who trust us to protect their families and power their properties. Our team consists of PSIRA-registered security technicians and ECSA-licensed electricians who take genuine pride in their work.

We use only quality equipment from trusted brands, follow SANS standards on every installation, and never cut corners. Every project — from a single gate motor to a full estate security system — receives our full attention, detailed planning, and rigorous testing before handover.

Today, Motsebone is one of Midrand's most trusted names in integrated security and electrical solutions. We are proud of the relationships we have built and the properties we have protected.
