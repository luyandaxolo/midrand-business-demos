What We Do

Complete Security & Electrical Services

From protecting your perimeter to powering your home, we handle every aspect with certified expertise.

CCTV Installation

HD & IP camera systems with remote viewing, night vision, and motion detection. Monitor your property from anywhere in the world.

Alarm Systems

Intruder detection, perimeter beams, and panic buttons. Wired and wireless solutions with armed response integration.

Access Control

Biometric, RFID card, and keypad entry systems. Secure your premises with audit trails and time-based access rules.

Electric Fencing

High-tension perimeter fencing with alarm integration. Fully compliant installations with energizer monitoring.

Intercom Systems

Audio and video intercoms for homes and estates. Remote gate release from your smartphone or indoor panel.

Gate Automation

Sliding and swing gate motors with battery backup. Smart remotes, GSM intercoms, and solar-powered options.

Electrical Repairs

Fault finding, circuit breaker replacements, rewiring, and general repairs. Qualified electricians for all residential and commercial work.

COC Certificates

Electrical Compliance Certificates for property sales, rentals, and insurance. Full inspection, testing, and official documentation.

Solar Installation

Grid-tied and off-grid solar systems. We size, supply, and install panels, inverters, and battery banks for your energy needs.

Backup Power

Inverter and battery backup systems for load shedding. Keep your lights, security, and essential appliances running during outages.
