Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things our clients ask.

How much does a garden design cost?

Our garden design fees depend on the size and complexity of your project. A typical residential garden design in Midrand starts from R2,500 for a basic layout and goes up to R8,000 for a full 3D visualisation with detailed planting plans. We always provide a clear, fixed quote before any work begins.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation quotes for all landscaping projects. We will visit your property, assess the work required, and provide a detailed written quote within 48 hours. For large projects, a small design deposit may be required, which is fully deducted from your final invoice.

What areas do you service?

We primarily serve Midrand and surrounding areas including Centurion, Sandton, Fourways, Johannesburg, Pretoria, Kyalami, Vorna Valley, Carlswald and Waterfall. For larger projects, we are happy to travel further across Gauteng.

How long does a typical landscaping project take?

Timeline varies by project scope. A lawn renovation or small planting job can take 1-2 days. A full garden redesign with hardscaping and irrigation typically takes 2-4 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule with your quote so you know exactly what to expect.

Do you provide maintenance after installation?

Absolutely. We offer flexible maintenance contracts ranging from weekly visits to monthly check-ins. Many clients choose our aftercare package to protect their investment and ensure their garden matures beautifully over time.

Are you insured?

Yes, Mondise Landscaping carries full public liability insurance and workers' compensation cover. You can have complete peace of mind when we work on your property.
