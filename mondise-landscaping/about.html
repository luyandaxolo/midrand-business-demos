Our Story

Mondise Landscaping was founded on a simple belief: every outdoor space has the potential to become something extraordinary. What started as a small garden maintenance operation in Midrand has grown into a full-service landscaping company trusted by homeowners, estates and businesses across Gauteng.

Over the past 10 years, we have completed over 200 projects — from intimate residential gardens to expansive commercial grounds. Our team combines deep horticultural knowledge with creative design thinking to deliver gardens that are not only beautiful, but also practical and sustainable in the South African climate.

We are proud to call Midrand home. Our understanding of local soil, indigenous plants, and Gauteng's unique weather patterns means we can recommend solutions that truly work. We do not believe in one-size-fits-all — every garden we design is as unique as the people who live in it.
