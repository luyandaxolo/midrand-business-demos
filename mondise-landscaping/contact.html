Common Questions

Quick answers to the things most people want to know before getting started.

How quickly can you start my project?

Typically we can begin smaller projects within 1-2 weeks. For larger garden redesigns, our lead time is usually 3-4 weeks depending on the season. We will always confirm our availability when we provide your quote.

Do you work on weekends?

We are open on Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM for consultations and smaller maintenance jobs. For larger projects requiring our full team, we schedule during weekdays to ensure the best results and safety.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept EFT, cash, and most major debit and credit cards. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit with the balance due on completion. Maintenance contracts are billed monthly.

Can you help with water-wise gardening?

Absolutely. Water conservation is one of our core priorities. We specialise in indigenous and drought-tolerant plant selections, efficient drip irrigation, and mulching strategies that reduce water use while keeping your garden beautiful.

Do you offer emergency tree services?

Yes, we provide emergency tree felling and storm damage response within 24 hours across our service area. If you have a dangerous tree or storm damage, call us immediately on 078 784 7282.

Prefer to Call?

Sometimes a quick conversation is the best way to start. We are happy to discuss your garden over the phone and arrange a site visit.
