Our Story

Who We Are

A locally owned business built on integrity, expertise, and a genuine commitment to our customers.

Kranz Compressor Components was founded with a simple mission: to provide Midrand businesses and factories with reliable, high-quality industrial compressor services at fair prices. Over the years, we have grown through word-of-mouth referrals and repeat customers who trust us to keep their compressed air systems running.

Our team consists of factory-trained technicians who take pride in their work. We use genuine OEM parts, follow manufacturer specifications, and never cut corners. Every repair, every installation, and every maintenance visit receives our full attention and commitment.

We understand that compressed air is the lifeblood of many manufacturing and industrial operations. When your compressor stops, your production stops. That is why we offer 24/7 emergency repairs and stock common parts for faster turnaround times.
