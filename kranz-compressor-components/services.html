What We Offer

Comprehensive Compressor Services

Every service is delivered by qualified professionals using quality parts and proven techniques.

Compressor Sales

We supply new and refurbished industrial compressors from Atlas Copco, Ingersoll Rand, Kaeser, CompAir, and other leading brands. Whether you need a rotary screw, piston, or oil-free compressor, we help you select the right machine for your production volume, air quality requirements, and budget.

Compressor Repairs

Our workshop handles everything from minor seal replacements to complete compressor overhauls. We repair motors, pumps, air ends, control systems, and dryers. Fast turnaround times mean less downtime for your factory or workshop.

Maintenance Contracts

Prevent costly breakdowns with scheduled preventive maintenance. We inspect, service, and replace wear parts before they fail. Our contracts include oil changes, filter replacements, belt checks, and full system diagnostics every 2,000 hours or 6 months.

Spare Parts & Components

We stock genuine OEM and quality aftermarket parts for all major compressor brands. From air filters and oil separators to pressure switches and valves, we have the parts you need to keep your system running. Hard-to-find parts sourced on request.

Installation & Commissioning

Professional installation of new and relocated compressor systems. We handle piping, electrical connections, dryer integration, and control setup. Proper commissioning ensures optimal performance, safety compliance, and full warranty coverage.

24/7 Emergency Repairs
