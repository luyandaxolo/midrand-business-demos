FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Do not see your question? Reach out and we will be happy to help.

How fast is your armed response?

Our average armed response time is under 10 minutes across Midrand. Response times vary slightly depending on traffic and your exact location, but we are continuously optimizing patrol routes to improve speed.

Do I need an existing alarm to get armed response?

No. We can install a new alarm system as part of your security package, or monitor and respond to most existing alarm systems. We support all major brands and offer free compatibility checks.

What areas do you cover?

We primarily serve Midrand, Kyalami, Vorna Valley, Halfway House, Blue Hills, Sunninghill, and Centurion. Contact us if you are just outside these areas—we may still be able to assist.

Are your officers PSIRA registered?

Yes, all our armed response officers are fully PSIRA-registered and undergo regular training in tactical response, first aid, and customer service.

How much does a security assessment cost?

Our initial security assessments are completely free and come with no obligation. We will visit your property, identify vulnerabilities, and provide a detailed recommendation report at no charge.

Can I get a quote without a site visit?

For simple packages, we can provide a rough estimate over the phone or WhatsApp. However, we recommend a free site visit for an accurate quote tailored to your property's specific risks and layout.

Prefer to Call or WhatsApp?

We are happy to discuss your security needs over the phone or WhatsApp. Reach out anytime during business hours for a free consultation.
