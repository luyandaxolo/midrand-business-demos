Our Security Services

Comprehensive protection solutions tailored to homes, estates, and businesses across Midrand and surrounding areas.

What We Offer

Detailed Service Overview

Every service is delivered by PSIRA-registered professionals with the latest equipment and training.

Armed Response

When your alarm triggers or you call for help, our armed response team is dispatched immediately. We maintain an average response time of under 10 minutes across Midrand, ensuring you are never left waiting when it matters most.

Our officers are PSIRA-registered, fully trained in tactical response, and equipped with the latest communication and safety gear. Whether it is a home invasion, break-in, or medical emergency, we respond with professionalism and urgency.

  • Average response under 10 minutes
  • PSIRA-registered tactical officers
  • GPS-tracked response vehicles

Alarm Monitoring

Our 24/7 control room monitors your alarm system around the clock. The moment an alarm is triggered, our operators assess the threat, verify the activation, and dispatch the nearest response vehicle instantly.

We support all major alarm brands and can upgrade legacy systems to modern, app-connected solutions. Receive real-time alerts on your phone and stay in control of your security from anywhere.

  • 24/7 live monitoring
  • Real-time mobile notifications
  • Compatible with all major brands

Panic Buttons

Wireless panic buttons provide instant access to emergency help at the press of a button. Ideal for elderly residents, vulnerable family members, or high-risk work environments. We install fixed and portable panic buttons throughout your property.

Each button is linked directly to our control room, bypassing any delays. Medical alert functionality is also available, ensuring help arrives fast in any emergency.

  • Wireless and portable options
  • Medical alert integration
  • Direct control room link

Patrol Services

Visible mobile patrols are one of the most effective deterrents against crime. Our marked response vehicles conduct regular patrols of residential estates, office parks, and industrial sites throughout Midrand.

Patrol officers check for suspicious activity, verify access points, and provide a rapid on-site presence if an alarm is triggered. Custom patrol schedules are available to suit your needs.

  • Marked, GPS-tracked vehicles
  • Custom patrol schedules
  • Estate and commercial coverage

Security Assessments

Not sure where your vulnerabilities lie? Our free on-site security assessments identify weak points in your property's perimeter, access control, and surveillance coverage. We provide a detailed report with prioritized recommendations.

Whether you are moving into a new home or reviewing your current security, our experts help you make informed decisions that match your risk profile and budget.

  • Free on-site evaluation
  • Detailed risk report
  • Budget-friendly recommendations

Emergency Dispatch

In critical situations, every second counts. Our emergency dispatch team coordinates directly with SAPS, emergency medical services, and fire departments to ensure a comprehensive multi-agency response when needed.

We do not just respond to alarms—we manage the entire emergency scene until the situation is resolved and you are safe. Our officers are trained in first aid and trauma management.

  • Direct SAPS liaison
  • First aid trained officers
  • Multi-agency coordination

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our security services.

How fast is your armed response?

Our average armed response time is under 10 minutes across Midrand and surrounding suburbs. Response times vary slightly depending on traffic and your exact location, but we are continuously optimizing our patrol routes to improve speed.

Do I need an existing alarm system?

No. We can install a new alarm system as part of your security package, or monitor and respond to most existing alarm systems. We support all major brands and offer free compatibility checks.

What areas do you cover?

We primarily serve Midrand, Kyalami, Vorna Valley, Halfway House, Blue Hills, Sunninghill, and Centurion. Contact us if you are just outside these areas—we may still be able to assist.

Are your officers PSIRA registered?

Yes, all our armed response officers are fully PSIRA-registered and undergo regular training in tactical response, first aid, and customer service.

Can I get a security assessment before signing up?

Absolutely. We offer free, no-obligation security assessments for homes and businesses in Midrand. We will identify vulnerabilities and provide a tailored proposal at no cost.

Do you offer armed response without alarm monitoring?

Armed response is most effective when paired with alarm monitoring, but we can discuss standalone options such as panic button-only packages. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
