Our Mission

Protecting Midrand, One Home at a Time

Tincol Security was founded with a single purpose: to give Midrand residents and businesses the security they deserve. In a community where safety concerns are real, we provide a professional, reliable, and rapid armed response service that you can count on—24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Every member of our team is PSIRA-registered, rigorously trained, and deeply committed to the communities we serve. We do not just respond to alarms; we build lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, transparency, and results.

From our state-of-the-art control room to our GPS-tracked response vehicles, we invest in the technology and training that keeps you safe. Our under-10-minute average response time is not a marketing claim—it is a standard we live by.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Proven by Results

Tincol Security was born from a simple observation: Midrand needed a security company that treated every client like family, not a number.

Starting with a single response vehicle and a commitment to under-10-minute response times, we have grown into one of Midrand's most trusted security partners. Our control room monitors hundreds of homes and businesses across Kyalami, Vorna Valley, Halfway House, Blue Hills, and beyond.

We have invested in modern technology—GPS-tracked vehicles, real-time alarm monitoring, mobile panic buttons, and integrated CCTV systems—to ensure our clients are protected by the best tools available.

But technology is only half the story. Our people are what set us apart. Our response officers, control room operators, and management team are all deeply committed to the safety and satisfaction of every client. We are not just a security company; we are your neighbours, and your safety is personal to us.
