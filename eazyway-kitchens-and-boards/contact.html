Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your kitchen project.

How long does a typical kitchen project take?

From design approval to installation, most projects take between 4 and 8 weeks. Complex renovations or large kitchens may take slightly longer. We always provide a clear timeline before starting.

Do you offer a free design consultation?

Yes. We offer a free, no-obligation consultation where we visit your home, measure your space, and discuss your vision. There is no pressure to commit — we are here to help you explore what is possible.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Midrand and surrounding areas including Kyalami, Vorna Valley, Halfway House, Blue Hills, and the greater northern Johannesburg region. Contact us to confirm if we cover your area.

What is included in your 10-year warranty?

Our 10-year warranty covers all cabinetry against manufacturing defects, including board warping, joint separation, and hardware failure under normal use. It does not cover damage caused by misuse, water leaks, or third-party alterations.

Can you work with my existing appliances?

Absolutely. We design around your existing appliances or can recommend and source new ones if you prefer. Our designers ensure every appliance fits perfectly within your new cabinetry.

Do you handle plumbing and electrical work?

For full renovation projects, we coordinate all required plumbing, electrical, and tiling work through our trusted network of licensed tradespeople. You deal with us — we manage the rest.
