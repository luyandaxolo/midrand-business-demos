Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Still unsure? Just call us — we're happy to talk.

How do I get a quote?
Simply call us, send a WhatsApp, or fill out the contact form on this page. For most projects, we can provide a rough estimate over the phone after a brief discussion. For larger jobs, we'll schedule a free site visit to assess the scope and provide a detailed, itemised quote within 24 hours.
Do you charge for quotes?
No. All quotes are completely free and carry no obligation. We believe you should have all the information you need to make the right decision without any pressure or upfront cost.
What areas do you cover?
We are based in Midrand and primarily serve Midrand, Centurion, Pretoria, Sandton, Randburg, and surrounding Gauteng areas. For larger commercial projects, we may travel further — contact us to discuss your location.
How long will my project take?
Project timelines vary by scope. A standard roof repair might take 1-2 days, while a full roof replacement or large paving job could take 1-2 weeks. We provide a clear timeline in every quote and keep you updated throughout.
Do you offer payment plans?
For projects over R50,000, we can arrange a phased payment schedule tied to project milestones (deposit, mid-project, completion). Smaller jobs are typically settled on completion. We accept cash, EFT, and major credit cards.
What happens if something goes wrong after the work is done?
Every project is backed by our 5-year workmanship guarantee. If you experience any issues related to our installation or repair work, simply contact us and we'll return to assess and fix the problem at no additional cost.

Need Urgent Help?

Storm damage? Roof leaking? We prioritise emergency repairs. Call us now for rapid response.
