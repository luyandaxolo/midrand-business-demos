Our Story

MaTe Business Group was founded over 20 years ago with a simple belief: every property owner deserves construction work they can trust. What started as a small roofing crew in Midrand has grown into a full-service construction company trusted by hundreds of homeowners, property managers, and businesses across Gauteng.

We've weathered economic downturns, material shortages, and industry changes — but our commitment to quality has never wavered. Every project, whether a single-room paint job or a complete roof replacement, receives the same attention to detail and pride in craftsmanship.

Today, our team includes skilled roofers, tilers, painters, waterproofing specialists, and structural engineers. We don't subcontract the core work — our own people handle your project from start to finish, ensuring accountability and consistency you won't find with fly-by-night operators.
