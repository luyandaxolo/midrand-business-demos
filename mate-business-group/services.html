Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you need, just call us.

How quickly can you start my project?
For most projects, we can begin within 3-7 days of quote acceptance. Emergency repairs (roof leaks, storm damage) are prioritised and can often be attended to within 24 hours. We'll always give you a realistic start date during our consultation.
Do you offer a warranty on your work?
Yes — every project comes with a 5-year workmanship guarantee. This covers installation defects and labour issues. Material warranties from manufacturers (typically 10-25 years for roofing) are also passed on to you in full.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. MaTe Business Group is fully registered, and we carry comprehensive public liability insurance as well as workers' compensation cover. Certificates of compliance are provided for all electrical and structural work where required.
Do you work on commercial properties as well?
Yes. While we serve many residential clients in Midrand and surrounding areas, we also undertake commercial projects including office waterproofing, retail paving, and industrial roofing. No project is too large or too small.
What areas do you serve?
We are based in Midrand, Gauteng, and primarily serve Midrand, Centurion, Pretoria, Sandton, Randburg, and surrounding areas. For larger projects, we may travel further — just ask during your consultation.
Can I supply my own materials?
We recommend using our trusted suppliers to ensure material quality and warranty coverage. However, if you have specific materials you'd like to use, we can discuss this during the consultation and adjust our quote accordingly.
