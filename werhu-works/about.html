Our History

Workshop & House Calls

At Werhu Works, we understand that car trouble never happens at a convenient time. That is why we offer two flexible options: bring your car to our fully equipped workshop in Midrand, or let us come to you. Our team of experienced mechanics handles everything from routine oil changes to complex engine rebuilds.

We started with a simple mission: make car care honest, convenient, and affordable for Midrand residents. No more waiting rooms. No more surprise bills. Just quality workmanship done right the first time.

The Founder

Meet Grant Ngobeni

The hands and heart behind every repair at Werhu Works.

Grant Ngobeni

Founder & Lead Mechanic

Grant has spent over a decade building his craft across some of South Africa's most respected automotive brands. Before founding Werhu Works, he trained and worked at Bidvest McCarthy VW, where he mastered the intricacies of Volkswagen engineering, and later at Bidvest McCarthy Toyota and Hino (Pty) Ltd, where he specialized in diesel engines and heavy-duty vehicle maintenance.
