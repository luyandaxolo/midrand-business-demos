Our History

We Come To You

At Werhu Works, we know that sometimes cars break down and it can be a big problem. That's why we come to you to fix your car, wherever you are. Our team of mechanics is really good at what they do, and we want to make fixing your car as easy as possible for you.

The Founder

Meet Grant Ngobeni

Hi, I'm Grant Ngobeni, and I run Werhu Works. I've been working in different jobs for more than ten years, learning a lot along the way.

First, I worked with cars at Bidvest McCarthy VW, where I learned all about how they work and how to give great service. Then, I moved to Bidvest McCarthy Toyota and Hino (Pty) Ltd, where I fixed diesel engines and forklifts, even in tough places like the Shoprite Distribution Centre.

Before I got into cars, I worked with computers at Tarsus Technology Group and then helped people with online banking at ABSA Bank. I like solving problems and making customers happy.

Now, at Werhu Works, I do all kinds of house calls car services, like changing oil, fixing brakes, and checking cooling systems. I really care about doing a great job for everyone who comes to me for help. You can trust Werhu Works to take care of your car needs because I'm dedicated to giving you the best service possible.
