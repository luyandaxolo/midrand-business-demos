Oil Change Service

Keeping your engine running smoothly starts with clean oil. Our team drains old oil, replaces the filter with a quality part, and fills your engine with the correct grade synthetic or mineral oil. We also check and top up all other fluids and reset your service indicator.

What is Included:

  • Drain and dispose of old engine oil
  • Replace oil filter with quality part
  • Fill with correct grade synthetic or mineral oil
  • Check and top up other fluids (coolant, brake, power steering)
  • Reset service indicator where applicable
  • Visual inspection of belts, hoses, and leaks

Timing Chain Replacement

A rattling noise or check-engine light could mean timing chain trouble. We replace the chain, tensioner, and guide rails, then verify engine timing before road testing. Do not wait — a failed timing chain can destroy your engine.

What is Included:

  • Timing chain and tensioner replacement
  • Guide rail inspection and replacement
  • Cam and crank seal replacement
  • Engine timing verification
  • Oil change recommended with service
  • Road test before handover
Timing chain jobs are complex and may require a full day. We will assess your vehicle first.
Time4–8 hrs
Warranty12 months

Cambelt Replacement

A snapped cambelt can write off your engine. We replace the belt, tensioner, idler pulley, and water pump (if driven by belt), then set full engine timing and test drive before handover.

What is Included:

  • Cambelt / timing belt replacement
  • Tensioner and idler pulley replacement
  • Water pump replacement (if driven by belt)
  • Cam and crank seal inspection
  • Full engine timing setup
  • Test drive and final checks
Most manufacturers recommend cambelt replacement every 60,000–100,000 km. Check your manual.
Time3–6 hrs
Warranty12 months
