FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really come to my house?
Yes. We are a mobile mechanic service. We bring tools, parts, and expertise to your home, office, or even the side of the road in Midrand and surrounding areas.
What areas do you cover?
We primarily serve Midrand including Noordwyk, Vorna Valley, Kyalami, Halfway House, Blue Hills, Waterfall, and The Reeds.
Do you offer a warranty?
Most major repairs come with a 12-month warranty on parts and labour. Oil changes carry a 30-day warranty.
How do I pay?
We accept cash, EFT, and card payments on completion. We always quote upfront so there are no surprises.
Can I bring my car to your workshop?
Yes. We have a fully equipped workshop at La Maison Royale, 82 Tamboti Rd, Witpoort, Midrand.
