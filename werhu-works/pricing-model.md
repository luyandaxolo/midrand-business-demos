## Why This Works for SA Small Businesses

1. **Rand pricing** — no dollar confusion
2. **Monthly is cheap** — R349 is less than one tank of petrol. Easy sell.
3. **No technical jargon** — package names they understand
4. **Risk reversal** — offer a "7-day tweak guarantee" after launch
5. **Social proof** — show them the mechanic site, then say "I can do this for your salon too"

## How to Close the Deal

### Discovery Questions (use these every time):
1. "Where do most of your customers find you right now?"
2. "Have you ever Googled your business name—what comes up?"
3. "How many calls/leads do you think you lose because people can't find your info?"
4. "What happens when someone visits your current site—or do you even have one?"

### The Close:
> "For less than R400 a month—less than your coffee budget—you get a site that works while you sleep. Customers find you, trust you, and book. I handle the tech. You handle your business."
