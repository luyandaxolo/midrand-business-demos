About Signs4SA

Two decades of craftsmanship, innovation, and unwavering commitment to helping South African businesses get noticed.

Our Story

From a Small Print Shop to South Africa's Signage Leader

Signs4SA began in 2000 as a modest printing operation in Johannesburg with a single mission: to help local businesses look professional and get noticed. What started with business cards and flyers quickly evolved as customers asked for bigger, bolder solutions.

Today, we are one of South Africa's fastest-growing signage companies — a BEE Level 1 certified, Pan-African manufacturer with a state-of-the-art production facility in Midrand and installation teams across Gauteng, the Western Cape, and KwaZulu-Natal.

Our in-house capabilities span design, large-format printing, CNC routing, LED electrical work, and professional installation. This means we control quality at every step and can deliver complex projects on tight timelines that competitors simply cannot match.

We have had the privilege of serving over 500 businesses — from startups and family restaurants to national retailers, construction firms, and government departments. Every project, regardless of size, receives the same meticulous attention to detail.

Our Values

What Drives Us Every Day

These principles are the foundation of every sign we design, every print we produce, and every relationship we build.

Quality Without Compromise

We use only premium materials — UV-resistant vinyls, automotive-grade wraps, and SABS-compliant substrates. If it does not meet our standards, it does not leave our workshop.

Integrity & Transparency

Clear quotes, honest timelines, and no hidden costs. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, not one-time transactions.

Speed & Reliability

We understand that signage is often business-critical. Our 24–48 hour express service and reliable delivery commitments mean your project happens on time, every time.

Innovation & Creativity

Signage is both a science and an art. Our designers stay ahead of industry trends, from LED neon effects to sustainable materials, ensuring your brand looks modern and fresh.

Customer-First Approach

Your success is our success. We offer free design consultations, unlimited revisions on mockups, and dedicated project managers who keep you informed at every stage.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to reducing waste through efficient production methods, recyclable substrates where possible, and energy-efficient LED technology in all illuminated signs.
