FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Don't see your question? Reach out and we'll be happy to help.

How long does a vehicle wrap take?

A full vehicle wrap typically takes 2–3 working days. Partial wraps and magnetic signs can often be completed within 24 hours. We also offer express fleet branding for urgent commercial needs.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, every project includes a free design consultation. Our in-house designers can create your artwork from scratch or refine existing designs. We provide mockups and 3D renders before production begins.

What file formats do you need?

We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) for the best print quality. We can also work with high-resolution PNG or JPG files (300dpi minimum at print size). If you're unsure, send us what you have and we'll advise.

Do you handle council approvals?

Yes, we prepare all necessary documentation including engineering drawings, sign location plans, and municipal application forms. We have extensive experience navigating Johannesburg, Pretoria, and Ekurhuleni council approval processes.

What is your warranty policy?

All signage comes with a minimum 12-month workmanship warranty. LED illuminated signs carry a 2-year warranty on electrical components. Vehicle wraps using premium cast vinyl are rated for 5–7 years with proper care.

Can you deliver outside Gauteng?

Absolutely. As a Pan-African manufacturer, we deliver and install across South Africa and neighboring countries. We also ship smaller items nationwide via courier with secure packaging.

Prefer to Call or WhatsApp?

We're happy to discuss your needs over the phone or WhatsApp. Reach out anytime during business hours for immediate assistance.
