FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have got answers. If you do not see what you need, just call us.

How quickly can you get to me?
We aim for 20–40 minutes anywhere in Midrand, depending on traffic and your exact location. Kyalami, Vorna Valley, and Waterfall Estate are typically under 25 minutes.
How much does towing cost?
Local towing in Midrand starts from R700. Accident recovery and long-distance towing are quoted based on distance and vehicle type. We always quote upfront — no hidden fees.
Can you tow my motorbike?
Yes, absolutely. We have specialised motorcycle towing equipment and trained operators who understand how to secure bikes without causing damage.

Yes, we work with all major insurers for accident towing and recovery. We can handle the paperwork and direct billing where your policy allows.

No. We are a 24/7 service and our pricing is consistent day and night, weekday and weekend. The price we quote is the price you pay.
