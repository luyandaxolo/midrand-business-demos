Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Service

B&C Towing Services started with a simple mission: to provide Midrand drivers with a towing company they could actually rely on. No hidden fees. No long waits. No surprises.

Over the years, we have grown from a single truck to a full fleet serving every suburb in Midrand — from Kyalami estates to Waterfall corporate parks, from Vorna Valley homes to Halfway House industrial areas. But our core values have never changed.

We know Midrand's roads, estates, and shortcuts. We know how stressful a breakdown can be. And we know that the difference between a good tow and a bad one is often just a friendly face, a fair price, and a truck that shows up when promised.

Our Values

What We Stand For

These are not just words on a page. They are the principles that guide every decision we make and every tow we perform.

Speed Matters

When you are stranded, time is everything. We obsess over response times because we know what it feels like to wait on the side of the road.

Honest Pricing

We quote upfront and stick to it. No surprise charges, no after-hours premiums, no hidden fees. Ever.

Safety First

Your vehicle and your safety are our top priorities. Every operator is trained, licensed, and insured for complete peace of mind.

Community Care

We are Midrand locals. We know the area, we know the people, and we treat every customer like a neighbour — not a transaction.

Professional Excellence

From our trucks to our training, we invest in quality. A well-maintained fleet and skilled operators mean fewer delays and safer tows.

Always Available

Breakdowns do not follow a schedule. That is why we are here 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Always.
