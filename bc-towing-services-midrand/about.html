Our Story

Who We Are

A locally owned business built on integrity, expertise, and a genuine commitment to our customers.

B&C Towing Services Midrand was founded with a simple mission: to provide Midrand residents and businesses with reliable, high-quality towing / roadside assistance services at fair prices. Over the years, we have grown through word-of-mouth referrals and repeat customers who trust us to get the job done right.

Our team consists of qualified professionals who take pride in their work. We use quality materials, follow industry best practices, and never cut corners. Every project — big or small — receives our full attention and commitment.
